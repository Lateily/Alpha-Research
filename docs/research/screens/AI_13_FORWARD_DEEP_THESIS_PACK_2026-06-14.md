### 1. 工业富联 `601138.SH`

**Thesis**

1. Industrial Fulian is the direct AI server / rack-scale ODM exposure in A-shares. The market already knows the story, but committed valuation still screens cheaper than most AI hardware peers.
2. The variant is not "AI servers are hot"; it is that earnings are catching up faster than the headline multiple. Q1 profit growth was extremely strong, and committed PE is 33.0x, with forward estimates implying a materially lower multiple if FY26 profit reaches the 500-600亿 range.
3. The hard question is margin: AI rack volume is real, but ODM economics can cap upside if cloud-server gross margin remains thin.

**Catalysts**

- 2026 H1 report: whether Q1 profit run-rate persists.
- GB300 / next-gen AI server delivery cadence in 2026H2.
- North American cloud capex guidance and order visibility.

**Valuation**

- Ref price: **70.57**.
- Committed PE: **33.04x**.
- Valuation frame: if FY26 profit reaches 500-606亿, forward PE is roughly 23-28x; this is the main reason it sits in first tier.
- Research entry zone: **<= 60**. That approximates a more defensible forward multiple and gives better upside to the external target zone around the low-90s.

**Risk / Wrong-if**

- H1 annualized net profit fails to approach ~450亿 run-rate.
- Cloud / AI server gross margin fails to hold near or above ~6%.
- Customer concentration and ODM bargaining power keep margins structurally capped.

**Trading Strategy / Research Posture**

- **No chase above 70.** At current ref price, this is a constructive watch, not a full starter.
- If price enters <=60 and no wrong-if fires, it can be considered for a tiny beta tracking starter, subject to human confirmation.
- Add only after H1 proves profit quality; reduce/exit if H1 says the profit run-rate was a one-quarter spike.
