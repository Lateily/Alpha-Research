## 2. Three-Point Thesis

### 2.1 What Is Real

The AI-related line is real, but smaller than the market narrative implies.

- 2025 年公司通信线缆业务收入 `25.51 亿`,同比 `+49.83%`.
- 其中高速通信线收入 `10.17 亿`,同比 `+237.99%`.
- 2025H1 通信线缆收入 `12.46 亿`,同比 `+62.46%`;其中高速通信线收入 `4.66 亿`,同比 `+397.80%`.
- 公司披露 224G 高速通信线持续稳定交付,448G 样品交付重点客户验证,PCIe 7.0 产品已开发。

**Interpretation:** 高速线确实已经从概念变成收入,不是纯题材。但集团 2025 收入 `84.51 亿`,高速线 `10.17 亿`只占约 `12.0%`;因此它是“重要第二曲线”,不是“已经重构整个集团”的主业。

### 2.2 What The Market May Be Mispricing

The market prices it as a direct AI copper-cable beneficiary, while the actual disclosure supports a narrower claim: **indirect AI copper interconnect supplier with optionality**.

- 公司披露“数据中心、人工智能、高性能计算”需求推动通信线缆增长。
- 公司没有在 A 股年报中披露 Nvidia 直供、Amphenol 直供比例、AI 客户收入占比。
- 前五大客户合计销售占比 `18.41%`,第一大客户 `8.70%`,未显示单一 AI 客户支配收入。

**Variant:** If H1 confirms high-speed line keeps compounding while group margin stabilizes, the market can justify treating 沃尔 as a lower-multiple AI interconnect proxy. If not, the stock is just a modestly valued diversified materials company with a hot sub-line.

### 2.3 What Keeps It From Being A Starter Today

The consolidated P&L is already warning that high-speed growth is not enough by itself.

- 2026Q1 收入 `20.32 亿`,同比 `+15.52%`.
- 2026Q1 归母净利 `2.31 亿`,同比 `-7.63%`.
- 2026Q1 扣非归母 `2.21 亿`,同比 `-8.20%`.
- 2026Q1 综合毛利率 `30.46%`,同比下降 `2.07pp`.
- 公司解释为高毛利电力高压产品和风电业务收入下降、原材料价格尚未传导、费用投入增加、外币汇率下降导致财务费用增加。

**Interpretation:** The bull case needs two things, not one: high-speed communication growth **and** consolidated margin recovery. Q1 only proved revenue growth, not earnings leverage.

## 6. Wrong-If Conditions

1. **Revenue proof fails:** FY2026 high-speed communication line revenue does not exceed `12-13 亿` or H1 run-rate shows sharp slowdown versus 2025H1.
2. **Margin proof fails:** consolidated gross margin remains below `31%` while revenue grows, showing AI mix cannot offset legacy pressure.
3. **Earnings proof fails:** H1 or FY2026 net income does not recover from Q1's negative YoY trajectory.
4. **Disclosure proof fails:** company still gives no customer/order evidence for AI data-center pull-through, leaving the thesis dependent on concept extrapolation.
5. **Technology substitution accelerates:** CPO/optical interconnect adoption compresses copper-cable TAM faster than expected.
